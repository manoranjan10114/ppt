CSM STRATEGY · Retention as a revenue lever

Win Retention via CSM Excellence

Reducing churn from 30% → 15% recovers ₹35L+/year — the single highest-ROI action we can take without spending on new acquisition.

1

📊 Account Health Scoring

  • AI-driven health score per account (usage, logins, support tickets)
  • Red / Amber / Green risk segmentation
  • Churn alerts triggered 30–45 days before renewal
  • CSM dashboard with live health view
2

🔄 Proactive Engagement Playbook

  • Structured onboarding — first 30 days critical
  • Monthly check-in cadence for all accounts
  • Automated touchpoints between renewals
  • QBR (Quarterly Business Review) for top accounts
3

🎯 Renewal & Upsell Engine

  • Renewal pipeline tracked 90 days in advance
  • Risk-based prioritization — CSM time on high-risk accounts
  • Upsell triggers tied to product usage milestones
  • Target: 20%+ upsell rate from existing base
4

📈 CSM Performance Metrics

  • Each CSM owns: churn %, renewal rate, upsell %
  • Weekly team health review — flag at-risk accounts
  • Account segmentation by risk tier & revenue size
  • Incentives tied to retention, not just new sales

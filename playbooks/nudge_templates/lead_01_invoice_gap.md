## DAY 0 — PHONE CALL (Primary)

> Assign to senior reps only. This is a closing call, not a discovery call.

**Script:**

> "Hi [Name], this is [Rep Name] from [Company].
>
> I'm calling because I can see your account is all set up and ready to go — you chose the [Plan Name] plan back in [Month]. It looks like the final invoice step didn't get completed.
>
> I wanted to check — was there something that came up, or did it just slip through? I can help you finish the setup right now on this call. It'll take about 2 minutes.
>
> [If they say they changed their mind:]
> "Totally understand. Can I ask what changed? Since then, we've actually simplified our pricing — plans now start at ₹1K/month, and our Booking Engine helps hotels save 18–25% on OTA commissions. Would it be worth a quick 5-minute catch-up on what's new?"
>
> [If they say timing:]
> "No problem at all. When would be a good time to get this going? I can hold your setup so you don't have to redo anything."
>
> [If they're ready:]
> "Great — I'm sending you the payment link right now. You should see it on WhatsApp and email in the next 30 seconds. Let me stay on the line while you complete it."

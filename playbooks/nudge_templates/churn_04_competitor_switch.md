# NUDGE TEMPLATES — Churned: Switched to Competitor (~80 Hotels)

> Hardest segment to win back. They made an active choice to go elsewhere.
> Don't hard-sell. Plant seeds. Lead with what competitors DON'T have.

---

## WHY THEY LEFT

- Competitor offered a better price
- Competitor had a specific feature we lacked
- Competitor's sales team was more aggressive
- Dissatisfied with our product/service and actively sought alternatives
- Peer recommendation for competitor

## WHY THEY MIGHT COME BACK

- Competitor may not have a Booking Engine (our key differentiator)
- Competitor's support may be worse (common complaint in the industry)
- Our new pricing may be competitive or lower
- They may be unhappy with the competitor after 6–12 months (honeymoon period ends)
- Free data migration removes the switching cost barrier

---

## APPROACH: SOFT TOUCH, NOT HARD SELL

These hotels chose to leave. Aggressive outreach will push them further away. The strategy is:

1. Acknowledge their choice respectfully
2. Share what's new (not what's "better")
3. Lead with the Booking Engine — the one thing most competitors don't have
4. Make it easy to come back if/when they're ready

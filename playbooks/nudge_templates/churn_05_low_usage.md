# NUDGE TEMPLATES — Churned: Low Usage / Silent Churn (~54 Hotels)

> These hotels just stopped using the product. No complaint, no specific issue.
> They faded away. The product didn't become essential to their daily operations.

---

## WHY THEY LEFT

- Product didn't become part of their daily workflow
- They didn't see enough value to justify continued payment
- Staff didn't adopt it — owner signed up but team didn't use it
- They managed bookings manually or through OTAs directly
- No one from our side noticed they stopped using it (no health monitoring at the time)

## WHY THEY'LL COME BACK

- Booking Engine gives them a NEW reason to use the platform daily (direct bookings = money saved)
- New onboarding ensures staff adoption, not just owner signup
- Health monitoring means we'll catch disengagement early this time
- Simpler product experience with fewer confusing options

---

## KEY APPROACH

These hotels don't have a grudge — they just drifted away. The tone should be warm, curious, and value-focused. Don't apologize (they didn't complain). Instead, show them something NEW that makes the product worth using.

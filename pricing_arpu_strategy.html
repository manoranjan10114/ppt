MIGRATION · Moving 1,800 customers to new tiers

Migrate Without Churn

Phase 1: New Customers (Month 1)

  • All NEW signups go on new 3-tier pricing immediately
  • Old plans no longer available for new customers
  • Sales team trained on new pricing and pitch
  • ROI calculator ready for every conversation

Phase 2: Preview (Month 2–3)

  • Email to all existing customers: "Here's your new tier"
  • Show what they GET (not what changes)
  • CSMs have 1:1 conversations with every customer
  • Handle objections before migration happens

Phase 3: Migrate at Renewal (Month 4–6)

  • Migration happens at each customer's renewal date
  • No mid-contract changes — respect existing agreements
  • Customers paying LESS: 1 month free as transition incentive
  • Customers paying MORE: immediate migration (price drop = goodwill)

Phase 4: Sunset Old Plans (Month 6+)

  • All customers on new tiers
  • Old plans fully retired
  • Custom/enterprise deals reviewed individually
  • Annual pricing pushed aggressively (2 months free)
